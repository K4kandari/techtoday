TERRIARUM TV

Terrarium TV is an application which allows user to watch , stream and download free HD tv show and movies on various platforms.Terrarium TV has almost all kind tv shows and movies that can be watched anytime. Multi language subtitles facility is also provided in the application.Content (List of tv shows and and movies) is updated on regular basis.

Terrarium TV application is supporeted in all major operating system. To watch movies and tv shows on any device , user just need a good internet connection.To download Terrarium tv appication on your android device , user require 2 things : Android 4.0.1. or above and mx player (or and kind of video player).Terrarium TV application is not available on the google playstore. User need to download from an third party sites like apk mirror , 9 apps and many more.

The most common bug noticed in the application is subtitle is not properly synced with the video.

The final word,Terrarium TV is a great app to keep us updated about our favourite tv series and movies in todays busy world
