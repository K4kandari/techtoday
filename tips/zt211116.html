Google Assistant


Having a personal assistant at your service is one of the things that every person would love,
Here we talk about THE BEST Artificial Assistant in the world,
Google now is an AI (Artificial Intellegence) software that you would like to use more in your daily life.

Google now can simply be made active/enable by pressing and holding the home button in most devices.

Various tasks can be easily accomplished by Google Now,

1.GPS : Wanna go to any location?..Just enable google now and say route to nearest McDonalds's (Example),
Google Now will tell you the time you require to get there and the distance from your current location and a map indicating the route.

2.Weather : Wanna know if you'll need an umbrella to go out or not?...simply ask google now about today's weather forecast,
and yeah now you know if umbrella is required or not.

3.Clock : Enable google now and tell her to wake you up at a particular time of choice

4.Reminder : Now let your phone remind you of your date with a friend or a movie on Friday night.
So just set reminders for any events on Google Now and forget about it.

5.Sports : If you are a sports fan and cannot watch the match at your work place, Google Now is here to help.
Add the team you wish to follow under customise option of the Google Now and it will show you related information like the details of their current match on your screen.
